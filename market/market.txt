DESCRIPTION

A very simple and small utility that displays various tidbits of information about the device. Can be useful for
developers to quickly determine device metrics or for people who are just curious about the type of device they
have. Information displayed:
 Screen resolution
 Density of device (actual and what the device thinks it is)
 Mobile country / network codes
 Device model / brand / manufacturer / CPU type
 Locale
 SDK Version

If you have any other suggestions please contact us at support@synaptik.com.
